Give yourself advice

Imagine that a loved one is experiencing a difficult time. Write down the advice that you would prescribe them in this situation. Once complete, write down how you can give this advice to yourself in times of struggle. Describe how you will show yourself the same love and support that you provided for them.

Show gratitude

Write down 5 thing you are grateful for in general. This could be anything from your family, friends, sunny days, or your favorite tv show. Next write down 5 thing you are grateful for concerning your body specifically. For instance, how it allows you to play the sport you love.

Write yourself a letter

Write yourself a letter of encouragement. Actively try to show yourself compassion. If you had a difficult day/week, recognize that you are not alone, and all human beings go through challenging times. Write about what happened, good or bad, and the emotions that accompanied.
